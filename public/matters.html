Voting Matters

Vote as if your life depends on it. It probably does. - Justin Dart, Jr.

The fact is, voting matters. Elections choose the leaders who make decisions that affect our lives every day. Voting says that you care and want to have a say in the world around you. It's your right and your fight. So get to the polls and make a difference. Take part in your civic life and make your voice heard!

Disability Rights Mississippi is commited to promoting a higher quality of life for people with disabilities in Mississippi. Participating in civic life is a key part of a fully inclusive community. We want to help you exercise your right to vote. If you have questions about voting in your community please contact us.
